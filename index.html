FAQs

We thought about some questions you may have… Here goes…

Is there a limit on how much I can send or receive?
There is a limit to the maximum amount of money that can be exchanged on the platform in a day based on FINTRAC regulations.
Is Pesapeer Regulated?
Yes, we are regulated by FINTRAC and registered as a Money Service Business in Canada.
What if I need help?
We are available round the clock to respond to your inquiry. Email us at support@pesapeer.com
Does Pesapeer fix or peg my rates?
No, our platform gives maximum flexibility to users to determine their exchange rates.
How do I ensure the other party fulfills their part of the deal when we exchange money?
Pesapeer ensures that your money is safe and secure. Our platform provides a secured Escrow service to hold funds for both sender and receiver and only release them when both parties have completed their transfer to the Pesapeer platform.
Is Pesapeer free?
Since we allow maximum flexibility for rates we charge a token for every transaction completed on the platform.
